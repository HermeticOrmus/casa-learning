## Tips for Effective Captures

1. **Use YOUR analogies**: "It's like in [show/game you love]..." beats generic examples
2. **Be specific on projects**: Not "useful for work" but "I can use this in the auth flow of my app"
3. **Make it actionable**: The TRY NOW should be copy-paste ready
4. **Tag for retrieval**: Future you will thank present you
